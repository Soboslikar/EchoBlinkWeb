A revolution in local communication

Forget about mobile data, Wi-Fi networks, and expensive servers. EchoBlink creates a phone-to-phone network.

📡

Completely Offline

Uses the ultra-fast L2CAP Bluetooth protocol. Enter your interests, offers, or requests, and the radar will automatically connect you with compatible people in a cafe, square, or festival, even when mobile networks are completely congested.

🛡️

Privacy Guaranteed

No account creation, no passwords. The system works via unique hardware tokens. Your personal data never goes to the internet.

🔋

Smart Battery

Thanks to asymmetrical transmission and fast BLE 24-byte packets, the app can run all day in your pocket without overheating your device.

For Business Users

Turn passersby into guests.

Do you own a cafe, restaurant, or store? Forget about expensive printing of paper flyers that end up in the trash. With EchoBlink, create a digital flyer in seconds and send it for free to everyone who passes by your venue.

  • ✓ Internet-free network: The flyer is automatically transferred to nearby phones.
  • ✓ Chain reach: Every person who receives your flyer briefly (15 min) becomes an extension of your venue, completely anonymously and passively forwarding the offer to new passersby.
  • ✓ Precise analytics: Always know how many people saw your offer.

Safety beyond network coverage

If you need help in places where the mobile network is unavailable or congested (like concerts, festivals, or underground garages), EchoBlink jumps in with its SOS Radar. With one click, your phone starts broadcasting an emergency call for help to all EchoBlink devices in your immediate Bluetooth vicinity, enabling a quick response from people around you.

Be among the first.

Important note for Network Pioneers: EchoBlink is a local network (range up to 100 meters). At the very beginning, there's a high chance no other users will be nearby. We kindly ask for your patience – simply leave the app installed in the background and forget about it.
