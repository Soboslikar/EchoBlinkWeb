Turn passersby into guests.

Imagine walking into a cafe in your neighborhood. You order a coffee, and instantly, everyone around you knows what services you offer, or what you're looking to buy. EchoBlink makes this a reality by digitally distributing your flyer to everyone within a 100-meter radius.

  • ✓ Internet-free network: The flyer is automatically transferred to nearby phones.
  • ✓ Chain reach: Every person who receives your flyer briefly becomes an extension of your business, passing the offer to new people they encounter.
  • ✓ Dynamic Proximity: Advertise precisely where you are physically located right now, not to someone 150km away.

Safety beyond network coverage

If you need help in places where the mobile network is unavailable or congested (like concerts, festivals, or underground garages), EchoBlink jumps in with its SOS Radar. With one click, your phone starts broadcasting an emergency call for help to all EchoBlink devices in your immediate Bluetooth vicinity, enabling a quick response from people around you.
